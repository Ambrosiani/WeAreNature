Coyotes:
Coyotes can live anywhere that humans do, but are so secretive that we rarely see them. They have been documented in every county in Pennsylvania.
Spiders:
A typical house supports at least 10 different species of spiders. These tiny eight-legged roommates are essential insect and pest control.
